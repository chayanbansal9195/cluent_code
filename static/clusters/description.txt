unreliable
Customers who have higher Annual Income but spend very less.These customers are "Un-reliable" and this group should be given minimal discount.

potential
Customers who have average Annual Income but carefully spend in shopping. These are Potential Customers, this group should get maximum discount.(focus to increase their frequency)

champions
Customers who have good Annual income and spend a lot in shopping. These customers are Champions, and should get discount between 1st and 2nd category(maintain their frequency)

budgetCustomer
Customers have less Annual Income but spend a lot of in shopping. These are  Budget Customers. They are already spending the maximum they could.(maintain their frequency)

InactiveCustomers
Customers who have less annual income and don't shop a lot. These are Inactive Customers(don't focus much on these customers)
